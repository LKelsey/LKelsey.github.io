Biography

I am a PhD Researcher in the Supernova Group in the Department of Physics and Astronomy at the University of Southampton. My research promarily focuses on the host galaxies of Type Ia Supernovae in order to determine how the observed properties of the supernova are affected by the physical parameters of the host. This is vital to take into account when using Type Ia Supernovae as standard candles, as they can bias our understanding of cosmological parameters, such as the dark energy content driving the accelerating expansion of the universe.

As a student of The Data Intensive Science Centre in the South East Physics Network, DISCnet, I am provided with specialist training throughout my PhD in a range of interdisciplinary data intensive science skills.
